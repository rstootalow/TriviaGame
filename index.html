Arrested Development Trivia Game!

Instructions:

  • Click the button below to start the game
  • You will have 15 seconds to answer each question
  • If CORRECT, you will be notified of your correct answer and will be brought to the next question
  • If INCORRECT, you will be notified of your incorrect answer, shown the correct answer and brought to the next question
  • If TIME EXPIRES, you will be notified and will be shown the correct answer.
  • At the end of the game, you will be shown the number of correct and incorrect guess, and will have an option to replay the game
